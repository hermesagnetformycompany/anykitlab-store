# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Creators, small-business owners, coaches, beauty professionals, and service brands arriving primarily from Instagram or direct product links. They need professional, editable Canva content without hiring a designer or beginning from a blank page.

## Product Purpose

AnyKit Lab is a digital-template storefront. It helps visitors discover, preview, purchase, and receive niche-specific Canva template systems. Success means visitors quickly understand the product quality, trust the checkout process, find the right niche kit, and complete an order with minimal friction.

## Brand Personality

Inventive, capable, energetic. The brand should feel like a modern creative laboratory: tactile enough to feel human, precise enough to trust, and visually confident without becoming noisy.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic SaaS gradients, flat poster-only layouts, repetitive card grids, decorative dashboards, stock-template ecommerce styling, excessive glassmorphism, or animation that slows shopping. The Mobile Clinic frontend is not a visual reference; only its customer/admin workflows are relevant.

## Design Principles

1. Show the product in motion rather than explaining the internal business process.
2. Make the storefront feel crafted, while keeping shopping paths familiar and fast.
3. Use depth, motion, and asymmetry to guide attention, not to decorate every element.
4. Keep customer and admin experiences visually distinct but operationally connected.
5. Label prototype-only payment, verification, and delivery behavior honestly.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.1 AA contrast, keyboard-visible focus, semantic controls, 44px minimum touch targets, and a complete reduced-motion alternative. Motion must not block navigation or obscure product information. Mobile performance is a first-class constraint.
